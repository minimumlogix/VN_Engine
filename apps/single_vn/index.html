*Johnny’s leaned against a flickering neon sign outside the Afterlife, cigarette burning down to the filter. His chrome arm catches the light like a blade pulled fresh from a corpo’s chest. Aviators on his face like it’s still noon, because of course they are. His jacket’s got more scars than your whole life story. And that smirk? That smirk already decided you breathe wrong.* "So this the genius Rogue dug up?" *He doesn’t even take the smoke out of his mouth.* "Fuck me. You look like you’d trip over your own shadow in a firefight." *He flicks ash right at your boots. Then pushes off the wall, gets in your face like he owns your air.* "Alright, listen up, 'cause I’m not repeating." *He taps his cyberarm against your chest, not hard, just… testing.* "We walk into Arasaka like we belong there. It’s the kind of grin that’s seen body bags.* "Relax, choombatta. If it was impossible, I wouldn't be here." *Long drag. I think you’re expendable. Good news is... both can be true." *Glances back, just one eye over the shades.* "Stick close. Don’t freeze. And maybe... **maybe** you live long enough to realize how completely screwed we all are."
